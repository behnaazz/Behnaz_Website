i've wanted a personal site for a long time. somewhere quieter than linkedin,
softer than github, more mine than a notion page someone shared with me once.

so — here it is.

## what this place is for

think of it as a *garden*, in the sense that
[maggie appleton uses the word](https://maggieappleton.com/garden-history) —
something that grows in seasons, gets pruned, sometimes goes wild.

i'll keep posting:

- **thoughts** — half-formed essays, marginalia from papers, things i'm
  trying to understand.
- **recipes** — the ones i actually cook, with the small honest bits
  (like *"this never works on the first try"*).
- **projects** — research i'm proud of, code i've written, tiny tools.
- **field notes** — sketches, photos, walks.

## a few rules i'm setting for myself

1. write small things often. one paragraph counts.
2. publish before it's ready. polish in public.
3. nothing here has to be useful to anyone else.

> a garden doesn't have to perform. it just has to keep growing.

if you stumbled in here somehow — welcome. take your shoes off, have a cup
of something warm, look around.
